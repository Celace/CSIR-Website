Sanitation and Environmental Management Division (SEMD)

The SEMD conducts R&D in Technology Process and Products (TPP) particularly in Environmental Management Technologies and Innovation Research. Areas of R&D activities include biodigester efficiency (input-output characterization), comparative study of new digester designs, and generation of electricity from biogas as well as cosmetics product development.
